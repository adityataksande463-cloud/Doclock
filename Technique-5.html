#5

The Third Eye

5–15 minutes
third eye intuition seeker ceo overthinker

Essence

The space between the eyebrows is the ajna chakra – the seat of intuition, inner vision, and wisdom. By gently focusing here, the mind becomes one-pointed, and a door opens to perception beyond the senses.

How To Do It

  1. Sit comfortably with spine erect. Close your eyes.
  2. Take a few deep breaths to center yourself.
  3. Gently bring your awareness to the point between your eyebrows – the third eye.
  4. You may feel a slight pressure, tingling, or simply a sense of presence there.
  5. Don't stare or strain. Just rest your attention softly on that point.
  6. If thoughts arise, let them pass and return to the third eye.
  7. You can also imagine a soft indigo light or a star at that center.
  8. Continue for 5–15 minutes. When you finish, sit quietly for a moment before opening your eyes.

Why It Helps

  • 🧘 Seeker: Activates the sixth chakra, deepening meditation and inner vision.
  • 💼 CEO: Enhances intuition and clarity – valuable for decision-making.
  • 🤔 Overthinker: Pulls awareness away from mental chatter into a single point, calming the mind.
  • 👨‍🎓 Student: Improves concentration and access to inner knowing.
  • ❤️ Heart-oriented: Balances the feeling heart with intuitive insight.
